Welcome To MoViE TiMe

MoViE TiMe is a multi-platform, free software BitTorrent client that includes an integrated media player. The application provides a free alternative to subscription-based video streaming services such as Netflix. MoViE TiMe uses sequential downloading to stream video listed by several torrent websites, and third party trackers can also be added manually. The legality of the software depends on the jurisdiction.


Following its creation, MoViE TiMe quickly received positive media attention, with some comparing the app to Netflix for its ease of use.
